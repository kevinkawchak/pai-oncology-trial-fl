# v0.9.1 — Regulatory Submissions Platform Module

## Summary

Built the `regulatory-submissions/` extended platform module providing end-to-end regulatory submission management for AI/ML-enabled medical device and drug submissions across FDA, EMA, PMDA, and other global regulatory authorities. The module includes 6 core Python components (submission orchestration, eCTD compilation, multi-regulation compliance validation, automated document generation, regulatory intelligence, and submission analytics), 6 progressive example scripts, and 6 test files. Complements `clinical-analytics/` (v0.9.0) — where clinical-analytics handles upstream analytics (PK/PD, survival, risk stratification), regulatory-submissions handles downstream packaging (eCTD compilation, compliance validation, submission tracking). Both share common regulatory/privacy/data standards and integrate via structured report outputs.

## Features

- **Submission Orchestrator** (`submission_orchestrator.py`, 870+ LOC): Core workflow management with `SubmissionPhase`/`SubmissionType`/`WorkflowStatus`/`TaskPriority` Enums, task dependency resolution, milestone tracking, reviewer assignments, deadline management, and 21 CFR Part 11 audit logging. Includes `create_standard_510k_workflow()` factory for common submission patterns.

- **eCTD Compiler** (`ectd_compiler.py`, 670+ LOC): Electronic Common Technical Document compilation per FDA Technical Conformance Guide. `ECTDModule`/`DocumentType`/`ValidationLevel` Enums. Generates eCTD module structures (M1-M5), validates document completeness, produces XML backbone stubs, and computes SHA-256 checksums for document integrity.

- **Compliance Validator** (`compliance_validator.py`, 750+ LOC): Multi-regulation validation engine covering 9 regulatory frameworks: FDA 21 CFR 820/Part 11, HIPAA, GDPR, ISO 14971, IEC 62304, ICH E6(R3)/E9(R1), EU MDR 2017/745. Checklist-driven validation, gap analysis, remediation tracking, and compliance score computation with severity-weighted findings.

- **Document Generator** (`document_generator.py`, 930+ LOC): Template-driven generation of 6 document types: CSR synopses, statistical analysis plans, risk analysis reports (ISO 14971), software descriptions (IEC 62304), predicate comparisons, and AI/ML predetermined change control plans (PCCPs). Produces structured Markdown with section numbering, metadata, and revision tracking.

- **Regulatory Intelligence** (`regulatory_intelligence.py`, 700+ LOC): Multi-jurisdiction guidance monitoring across 7 authorities (FDA, EMA, PMDA, TGA, Health Canada, MHRA, NMPA). Tracks AI/ML device guidance, assesses impact on ongoing submissions, generates compliance timelines, monitors enforcement actions.

- **Submission Analytics** (`submission_analytics.py`, 680+ LOC): Submission quality KPIs including cycle time, deficiency rates, and first-pass yield. Trend analysis with improving/stable/declining classification. Benchmarking against FDA MDUFA performance goals and industry standards.

- **6 Example Scripts:** Progressive examples from basic submission workflow through full end-to-end pipeline with clinical-analytics integration.

- **6 Test Files:** Comprehensive coverage including at least one integration test verifying clinical-analytics to regulatory-submissions data flow.
