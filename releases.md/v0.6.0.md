v0.6.0 - Privacy Framework and Regulatory Compliance Infrastructure

## Summary

Builds the complete privacy and regulatory compliance infrastructure for the platform. The privacy framework adds 5 production-grade modules covering PHI/PII detection (all 18 HIPAA Safe Harbor identifiers), HMAC-SHA256 de-identification with cryptographic salt, role-based access control with 21 CFR Part 11 audit trails, breach response with four-factor risk assessment and 60-day notification tracking, and Data Use Agreement generation for 5 agreement types. The regulatory framework adds 4 modules plus a human oversight QMS document covering FDA submission lifecycle tracking (citing Jan 2025, Aug 2025, Feb 2026 guidance), IRB protocol management with consent versioning, ICH E6(R3) GCP compliance scoring, and multi-jurisdiction regulatory intelligence across FDA/EMA/PMDA/TGA/Health Canada. Total addition: ~5,400 lines of Python across 9 new modules, 12 README files, and 1 QMS document.

## Features

- **PHI/PII Detection Engine** (`privacy/phi-pii-management/phi_detector.py`) — All 18 HIPAA Safe Harbor identifiers per 45 CFR 164.514(b)(2) mapped to PHICategory Enum with risk classification (Critical/High/Medium/Low). Regex patterns for SSN, MRN, phone, email, dates, geographic data, IP, ZIP, account numbers, certificates, vehicle IDs. Conditional DICOM tag scanning (pydicom). Optional Presidio NER integration. Batch scanning with aggregated ScanResult. PHI values never logged in full — only redacted previews.
- **De-identification Pipeline** (`privacy/de-identification/deidentification_pipeline.py`) — Six transformation methods (REDACT, HASH, GENERALIZE, SUPPRESS, PSEUDONYMIZE, DATE_SHIFT). HMAC-SHA256 pseudonymization with 32-byte cryptographically random salt via os.urandom(32). Consistent patient-level date shifting derived from HMAC of patient_id. Geographic generalization (ZIP to 3-digit prefix per Safe Harbor). Complete transformation audit trail with per-field records.
- **Access Control Manager** (`privacy/access-control/access_control_manager.py`) — RBAC with 10 roles, 22 permissions, and 13 audit action types. 21 CFR Part 11 compliant immutable audit trail. Time-limited access with automatic expiration. Invalid date formats deny access by default (fail-closed). get_audit_log() returns a defensive copy. User lifecycle management (register, deactivate, reactivate, role change).
- **Breach Response Protocol** (`privacy/breach-response/breach_response_protocol.py`) — HIPAA Breach Notification Rule (45 CFR 164.400-414) implementation. Four-factor risk assessment per 45 CFR 164.402 with score clamping to [0.0, 1.0]. 60-day notification deadline tracking. Automatic notification generation (HHS OCR, state AG, individual, media for 500+ individuals, GDPR DPA). Remediation action tracking with verification workflow.
- **DUA Template Generator** (`privacy/dua-templates-generator/dua_generator.py`) — Five DUA types (intra-institutional, multi-site, commercial, academic, government). Security requirements tiered by agreement type (encryption AES-256, MFA, DLP, network segmentation, annual audits, background checks). Data retention policies with destruction certification. 9-section document structure.
- **FDA Submission Tracker** (`regulatory/fda-compliance/fda_submission_tracker.py`) — Five submission pathways (510(k), De Novo, PMA, Breakthrough, Pre-Submission). 12-state lifecycle. AI/ML component tracking with model_type defaulting to "unspecified". Standard document requirements per pathway. References FDA AI/ML Device Guidance (Jan 2025), PCCP Guidance (Aug 2025), QMSR (Feb 2026). Review cycle management.
- **IRB Protocol Manager** (`regulatory/irb-management/irb_protocol_manager.py`) — Protocol lifecycle with 11 states. Amendment management with automatic version incrementing. Consent versioning with AI/ML disclosure tracking per ICH E6(R3). Continuing review workflow. Expiration tracking with days-until-expiration calculation.
- **GCP Compliance Checker** (`regulatory/ich-gcp/gcp_compliance_checker.py`) — 13 ICH E6(R3) GCP principles including risk-proportionate approach. Five compliance levels. Compliance score excludes NOT_ASSESSED findings from denominator. Standard checklist with 45+ individual checks. Prioritized remediation recommendations.
- **Regulatory Intelligence Tracker** (`regulatory/regulatory-intelligence/regulatory_tracker.py`) — Five jurisdictions (FDA, EMA, PMDA, TGA, Health Canada) with built-in profiles. Regulatory update monitoring with priority classification. Compliance requirement tracking. get_overdue_requirements() checks OVERDUE before IMMINENT (correct ordering). Dashboard summary generation.
- **Human Oversight QMS** (`regulatory/human-oversight/HUMAN_OVERSIGHT_QMS.md`) — CRF auto-fill risk tiers (low: auto-populate; medium: draft + review; high: advisory only). AE automation boundaries (detection: auto; grading: draft; causality: advisory; submission: prohibited without e-signature). Safety gates (SAE immediate escalation, no autonomous regulatory submission, >95% AE detection sensitivity).
